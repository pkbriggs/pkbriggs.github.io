Projects
NoteMap (April 2015 - present)
NoteMap is a platform designed to allow programmers to better annotate and take notes on code. What began as a Stanford senior project assignment later went on to win an award at the Stanford senior project fair, and we are continuing to develop our product in hopes that students can begin to use it in their classes.

Tutera (September 2014 - December 2014)
Tutera is a mobile web-app created to solve the problem of connecting helpful tutors to hardworking students. The site was originally created for the class Human Computer Interaction and Design at Stanford, thus the focus of the project was on the process of needfinding, sketching, user testing, prototyping, and developing a final product.

TryBTC (April 2013 - September 2014)
TryBTC is a website with the goal of teaching anyone in the world, no matter their background, about Bitcoin in under 5 minutes. My roommate and I started TryBTC as a class project, but we continued to work on it and proceeded to launch it publicly and raise venture capital. TryBTC has since helped over 500,000 people learn about and use Bitcoin.
